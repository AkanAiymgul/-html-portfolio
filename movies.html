The best movies I have ever seen

Top 3

Ocean's 8 (2018)

Debbie Ocean, recently released from prison, gathers an all-female crew of experts to steal a $150 million diamond necklace during the Met Gala. With precise planning and clever deception, they navigate unexpected challenges to pull off the perfect heist.

Death on the Nile (2022)

Detective Hercule Poirot's peaceful vacation aboard a Nile River cruise turns deadly when a wealthy heiress is murdered. As tensions rise among the passengers, Poirot uncovers a web of love, jealousy, and betrayal to reveal the killer.

Murder on the Orient Express (2017)

A luxurious train ride turns into a thrilling mystery when an American passenger is murdered. Renowned detective Hercule Poirot must race against time to uncover the truth, discovering that every passenger harbors secrets tied to the crime.
